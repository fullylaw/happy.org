Dread Reborn

The most immersive and creepy modpack for Minecraft

This modpack is designed to keep you on edge while trying to navigate through pitch-black caves and foggy nights. Dread replaces every vanilla hostile mob with grotesque creatures that stalk and chase you through the dark.

Play with friends using proximity chat, but stay close—losing your group in the pitch-black could be your final mistake.

Version 3.0 is a complete overhaul. We've added immersive biomes, seasons, thirst mechanics, realistic physics, and a suite of new horrors.
