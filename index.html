Dread Reborn

The most immersive and creepy modpack for Minecraft

This modpack is designed to keep you on edge while trying to navigate through pitch-black caves and foggy nights, never allowing you to feel comfortable. DREAD is meant to make every task feel like it might be your last, it does this by removing every vanilla hostile mob and instead replacing them with grotesque creatures that stalk and chase the player.

Play with your friends using the included proximity chat mod, but make sure not to lose them or you might be alone in the pitch-dark.

"DREAD Reborn", which is the new version 3.0 of the modpack, changes everything about it to make it more immersive and horrifying than ever.

The same principles are here, but this overhaul adds so much more to the experience, such as: some guns, multiple main biomes, seasons, thirst, new shaders and textures, realistic physics, new horrors and more...

While there are some known bugs with 3.0, these will be quickly fixed
